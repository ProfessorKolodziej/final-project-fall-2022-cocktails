Brandy

36-60%

Brandy is a distilled spirit made from fruit-based wine. It has a lightly sweet taste with hints of flowers, fruit, citrus zest, and a slight oak flavor.

Gin

36-50%

Gin is a distilled spirit that is made from grain and flavored with botanicals. It is most noted for its pine flavor due to its main ingredient, juniper berries.

Rum

40%

Rum is a spirit distilled from the fermented juice of sugarcane and other sugarcane products. Even though it isn't inherently sweet, it tends to evoke the taste of sugar.

Tequila

35-55%

Tequila is a distilled liquor, usually clear in color and unaged, and made from the fermented juice of an agave plant. It can have spicy flavors such as pepper, cinnamon and baking spices.

Vodka

35-50%

Vodka is a distilled alcoholic beverage, clear and colourless and without definite aroma or taste. It is traditionally made from distilling liquid from fermented cereal grains, and potatoes.

Whiskey

40-40%

Whiskey is a type of liquor made from fermented grain mash. It has a rich, smooth, and full-bodied taste with an essence of spiced, sweet notes such as vanilla, zesty fruit and oak.
